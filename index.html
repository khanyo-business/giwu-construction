About Giwu Construction

Founded in 2021 by Nolukhanyo, Giwu Construction brings together expertise in project management, quantity surveying, construction health & safety, and on-site supervision. We manage projects efficiently and deliver excellent value while maintaining the highest standards of safety and compliance.

Founder & CEO

Nolukhanyo — Founder & CEO

Nolukhanyo is the founder and CEO of Giwu Construction. She holds an Advanced Diploma in Quantity Surveying (NQF L7), Advanced Diploma in Construction Health & Safety (NQF L7), Diploma in Building (NQF L6), NCV L4 Civil Engineering & Building Construction, and an NQF 8 Postgraduate Diploma in Construction. She also has strong academic and practical experience in project management, bringing leadership and technical excellence to every project.

Trust & Experience: Her multidisciplinary background in construction, safety, quantity surveying, and project management positions her as a reliable leader capable of delivering high-standard results with transparency, precision, and commitment.
