About Giwu Construction

Founded in 2021 by Nolukhanyo Ngceni, Giwu Construction brings together expertise in project management, quantity surveying, construction health & safety, and on-site supervision. Founder & CEO

Nolukhanyo Ngceni — Founder & CEO

Nolukhanyo Ngceni founded Giwu Construction in 2021. She holds an Advanced Diploma in Quantity Surveying (NQF L7), an Advanced Diploma in Construction Health & Safety (NQF L7), a Diploma in Building (NQF L6), NCV L4 in Civil Engineering and Building Construction, and now an NQF 8 Postgraduate Diploma in Construction. Nolukhanyo brings both academic excellence and on-site experience to every project.

Achievements & Trust: Nolukhanyo was one of the top students selected by Walter Sisulu University to attend the SACQSP conference and has strong credentials in quantity surveying and safety management. Giwu Construction is 100% black woman owned and operates with strict compliance to safety and quality standards — making us a reliable partner for both public and private sector projects.
